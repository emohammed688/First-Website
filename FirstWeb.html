About me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Esam Mohammed and I am a 20 year old college student from Niles IL. Born and raised in Chicago for most of my life, I attended elementary school in the North West side of the city up until 2009. In 2009 I left the U.S and went on to live in Jordan for about four years. In 2013 I returned back to the U.S and finished high school at Maine East High school in Park ridge IL. After completing high school I enrolled in my local community college to fulfill my general education requirements. My plan is to enroll at Depaul University this up coming fall to pursue a degree in Computer Science.

Why Web Development

Throughout my first year in college, I was unsure on what I wanted to major in or what I enjoyed doing. While I did not know what I wanted to do, I knew it had to be something I was passionate about. I spent the first year taking different classes just to see what I was interested in, but nothing caught my attention. It was not until my second year of school where I took my first CS class that I finally decided that I wanted to further my education in Computer science. Although my ultimate goal is to work in artificial intelligence, I believe web-development is the first step towards achieving that goal.
